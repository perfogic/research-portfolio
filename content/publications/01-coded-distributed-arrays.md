This entry is intentionally modest. I do not want to present CDA as a finished result. At this stage, it is better described as a problem-formation artifact: the first place where a sequence of reading topics condensed into a narrower research direction.

The reason I include it here is that it shows the transition from reading to synthesis. The central question is not whether coding can help. It is which retrieval and reconstruction assumptions are doing the real work once the system is no longer purely replication-based.
