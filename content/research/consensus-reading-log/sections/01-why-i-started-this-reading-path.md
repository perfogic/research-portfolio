---
title: "Why I started this reading path"
order: 1
---

I wanted a reading path that would force explicit comparison. Consensus papers often advertise improvements in rounds, communication, responsiveness, or practical performance, but those improvements are not commensurate unless the assumptions are carried along with them.

The path therefore began with a baseline and then branched by structural question:

- how leader handoff is preserved;
- how fast paths are justified;
- how Ethereum changes the abstraction;
- when leaderless coordination is worth the cost.
