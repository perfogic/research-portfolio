---
title: "How my idea evolved"
---

The shift was gradual:

1. blobs made the data layer visible;
2. DHT-style retrieval made the network bottleneck visible;
3. PeerDAS made verifiable availability the central abstraction;
4. RDA and RLNC made coding and recoverability the central pressure point;
5. CDA emerged when I started asking whether coded retrieval structure itself should be the object of design.

What changed most was not the mechanism I found interesting. It was the granularity of the question.
