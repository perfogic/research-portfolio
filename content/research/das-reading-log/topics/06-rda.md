## Human version

RDA sharpened the difference between checking that data is probably there and ensuring the data can actually be reconstructed.

## Why I studied it

At this stage I was no longer satisfied by availability language alone. I wanted the recovery question to be explicit.

## Key insight

Recoverability forces a stricter comparison. It prevents elegant availability claims from hiding weak retrieval assumptions.

## Why it mattered for CDA

This was one of the clearest bridges from reading to my own direction.
