## Human version

CDA is my current attempt to ask the retrieval question directly: what if the array structure and coded retrieval path are treated as the main design object?

## Why I studied it

By this point I was no longer only following other designs. I was trying to name the narrower problem that the reading path had made visible.

## Core mechanism

The current intuition is to reason in terms of coded arrays, retrieval paths, and reconstruction guarantees rather than only in terms of replication plus sampling.

## What confused me initially

I first thought the direction was mostly about choosing a coding technique. It is more about choosing the right abstraction for retrieval and recoverability.

## Key insight

The question is not "can coding help?" It is "what does the system actually assume about coded retrieval?"

## Outcome

This is still an early research attempt. I present it here as problem formation evidence, not as a finished answer.
